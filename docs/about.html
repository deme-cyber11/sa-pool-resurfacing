Our Story

Built on Quality, Driven by Results

Alamo Pool Resurfacing was founded on a simple belief: San Antonio homeowners deserve pool resurfacing that's done right the first time — with honest pricing, premium materials, and craftsmanship that lasts.

We understand the unique challenges Texas pools face. From extreme summer heat that cracks plaster to the limestone-rich soil that shifts foundations, our team has the specialized knowledge to handle what out-of-town contractors simply don't understand.

With hundreds of pools resurfaced across San Antonio and the Hill Country, we've built our reputation the old-fashioned way — one satisfied homeowner at a time. Every crew member is trained in-house and certified in the latest application techniques, because your pool deserves nothing less.
