San Antonio's Trusted Pool Resurfacing Experts

Alamo Pool Resurfacing was founded with a simple mission: deliver exceptional pool resurfacing at fair prices to San Antonio homeowners. For over 15 years, we've been transforming worn, stained, and damaged pools across the metro area — from the established neighborhoods of Alamo Heights to the growing communities of Stone Oak and beyond.

We're a family-owned business, and we treat every pool like it's our own. That means using premium materials, employing skilled craftsmen, and standing behind our work with a 7-year warranty. We don't cut corners, and we don't leave until you're completely satisfied.

Why We're Different

San Antonio's climate presents unique challenges for pool surfaces. The intense Texas sun, hard Hill Country water, and extreme summer temperatures accelerate wear on pool finishes. We've spent years perfecting our techniques specifically for these conditions.

Every member of our crew is trained in proper surface preparation — the most critical step in any resurfacing job. We don't rush the prep work, because we know that a beautiful finish starts with a properly prepared surface.

Our Commitment

  • Licensed and insured Texas contractor
  • Premium materials from trusted manufacturers
  • Experienced crews with years of hands-on training
  • Comprehensive 7-year warranty on all finishes
  • Free on-site estimates with honest recommendations
  • Clean job sites — we leave your yard better than we found it

Serving the San Antonio Community

We proudly serve homeowners across the entire San Antonio metro area, including Alamo Heights, Stone Oak, Helotes, Boerne, New Braunfels, Schertz, Converse, and all surrounding communities. Whether you have a small backyard pool or a large commercial facility, we have the experience and equipment to handle the job.
