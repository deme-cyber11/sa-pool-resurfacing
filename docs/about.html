About Us — San Antonio Pool Resurfacing Specialists

About Alamo Pool Resurfacing

Alamo Pool Resurfacing was founded over a decade ago by San Antonio native, Mark Rodriguez, with a singular vision: to bring unparalleled pool resurfacing and replastering services to his beloved hometown. Growing up amidst the vibrant culture and warm climate of San Antonio, Mark witnessed firsthand the joy a sparkling, well-maintained pool brought to families. He also recognized the unique challenges our local environment poses to pool integrity. From these deeply rooted local insights, Alamo Pool Resurfacing was born, built on a foundation of integrity, exceptional craftsmanship, and an unwavering commitment to the San Antonio community. We’re more than just a business; we’re your neighbors, dedicated to enhancing outdoor living spaces across the Alamo City, one beautiful pool at a time.

Our Mission

Our mission at Alamo Pool Resurfacing is to serve San Antonio, TX homeowners with the highest standard of pool resurfacing and replastering services. We are dedicated to transforming worn, outdated pools into stunning backyard oases through superior craftsmanship, durable materials, and meticulous attention to detail. We strive to provide a seamless, stress-free experience for every client, ensuring lasting quality and complete satisfaction. By upholding principles of transparency, reliability, and local expertise, we aim to be the most trusted and preferred partner for all pool renovation needs in San Antonio and its surrounding communities, enhancing both property value and family enjoyment.
