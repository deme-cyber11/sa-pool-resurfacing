What Is Pool Resurfacing and Replastering?

Pool resurfacing and replastering refers to the comprehensive process of removing your swimming pool's deteriorated interior finish and applying a brand-new, durable surface. This involves several meticulous steps: first, the pool is completely drained, and the existing plaster is chipped away or hydro-blasted to expose the underlying shell. Any necessary repairs to the shell, such as crack patching, are performed. Next, a bond coat is often applied to ensure optimal adhesion for the new finish. Finally, the chosen new material – whether traditional white plaster, quartz aggregate, pebble finishes, or polished aggregates – is skillfully applied, troweled smooth, and allowed to cure. These modern materials offer superior longevity, chemical resistance, and an array of aesthetic options, transforming your pool's look and feel while providing a smooth, non-abrasive surface that is both technical in its application and accessible in its daily use.

Benefits of Professional Pool Resurfacing and Replastering

  • Enhanced Durability & Longevity: A professionally applied new surface protects your pool's shell from water damage, chemical corrosion, and UV degradation, dramatically extending its functional lifespan. This reduces the need for frequent repairs and ensures your investment lasts for many years.
  • Improved Aesthetics & Property Value: Resurfacing instantly revitalizes your pool's appearance, turning an aging, stained, or cracked surface into a pristine, inviting feature. This modernization significantly boosts your property's curb appeal and overall market value in the competitive San Antonio housing market.
  • Enhanced Safety & Comfort: Rough, pitted, or chipped plaster can be abrasive, causing scrapes and discomfort for swimmers. A smooth, new finish eliminates these hazards, providing a comfortable, safe, and enjoyable swimming experience for everyone who uses your pool.
  • Better Water Chemistry & Hygiene: Old, porous plaster is a breeding ground for algae and makes it challenging to maintain balanced water chemistry. A new, non-porous surface resists algae growth, simplifies cleaning, and allows for more stable chemical levels, leading to healthier, clearer water.

Common Questions About Pool Resurfacing and Replastering

How much does Pool Resurfacing and Replastering cost in San Antonio, TX?

The cost for pool resurfacing in San Antonio, TX, typically ranges from $4,000 to $15,000+, depending on the pool's size, the type of finish chosen (e.g., standard plaster, quartz, pebble), and the extent of any necessary repairs to the shell. While basic white plaster is generally the most economical, premium finishes offer enhanced durability and aesthetics for a higher investment. We provide a detailed, customized estimate after a thorough on-site assessment.

How long does Pool Resurfacing and Replastering take?

The actual application process for pool resurfacing usually takes about 3 to 7 days, depending on the pool's size and the finish chosen. However, the entire project timeline, including draining, preparation, application, initial filling, and crucial curing time (which can take 2-4 weeks with regular brushing and chemical balancing), typically spans several weeks from start to finish. We always aim for efficiency without compromising quality.

How do I know if I need Pool Resurfacing and Replastering?

Several clear signs indicate your pool may need resurfacing. These include a rough, abrasive texture on the pool floor or walls that can scrape skin, visible cracks or delamination of the plaster, persistent staining that won't come clean, exposed aggregate beneath the surface, or chronic algae problems even with proper chemical treatment. If your pool is constantly losing water due to small leaks in the shell, resurfacing can often address these issues as well.
