Frequently Asked Questions

Expert answers to common pool resurfacing questions

How much does pool resurfacing cost in San Antonio? +
Pool resurfacing typically costs $4,000-$15,000 depending on pool size and finish type. Basic plaster runs $4-6 per square foot, while premium pebble finishes cost $8-12 per square foot. Quartz finishes fall in the middle at $6-10 per square foot. We provide free on-site estimates with exact pricing for your specific pool.
How long does pool resurfacing take? +
Most pool resurfacing projects take 5-7 days from start to finish. This includes draining (1 day), surface preparation (1-2 days), applying the new finish (1 day), filling (1 day), and initial chemical balancing. Weather can occasionally affect timing, but we'll keep you informed every step of the way.
When is the best time to resurface a pool in San Antonio? +
October through March is ideal for pool resurfacing in San Antonio. Cooler temperatures allow for better curing of the new surface, and you won't miss prime swimming season during the hot summer months. However, we resurface pools year-round with proper precautions during extreme heat.
How long does pool plaster last in Texas? +
Standard pool plaster lasts 7-12 years in Texas's intense climate. Pebble finishes last 15-20+ years due to their superior durability. Quartz finishes typically last 12-15 years. Proper water chemistry maintenance extends the life of any finish significantly.
What are signs my pool needs resurfacing? +
Common signs include: rough or sandpaper-like texture that's uncomfortable on feet, visible staining that won't clean with chemicals, surface cracks or delamination, exposed aggregate or pebbles popping out, persistent algae growth in porous areas, and chalky white residue when you rub the surface. If you notice any of these, it's time for a professional assessment.
Can I resurface my pool in summer in San Antonio? +
Yes, we resurface pools year-round. However, extreme heat above 100°F requires special precautions including early morning application times and misting to prevent too-rapid curing. Fall and winter provide ideal curing conditions, but if you need it done in summer, we know how to handle the Texas heat.
Pebble vs plaster - which should I choose? +
Plaster is more affordable ($4-6/sq ft) with a 7-12 year lifespan. Pebble costs more upfront ($8-12/sq ft) but lasts 15-20+ years and handles San Antonio's hard Hill Country water better. For long-term value in Texas, pebble is usually the better investment. We're happy to discuss both options during your free estimate.
Do you offer financing? +
Yes, we offer flexible financing options to make pool resurfacing affordable for every budget. Ask about our 0% interest options for qualified buyers. We also accept cash, check, and all major credit cards.
How soon can I swim after resurfacing? +
For plaster finishes, you can typically swim after 7-10 days once the water chemistry is properly balanced. Pebble and quartz finishes may allow swimming in as few as 5-7 days. We'll provide specific guidance based on your finish type and current conditions.
Do you handle commercial pools? +
Absolutely. We resurface commercial pools including HOA community pools, hotel pools, apartment complex pools, and municipal facilities across the San Antonio metro area. We work closely with property managers to minimize downtime and complete projects on schedule.
