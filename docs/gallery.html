Types of Projects We Complete in San Antonio, TX

  • Standard Residential Pool Resurfacing: We specialize in bringing older San Antonio residential pools back to life. This often involves stripping worn plaster, addressing any structural issues, and applying a fresh, durable new finish that withstands the Texas heat and heavy use for years to come.
  • Commercial Pool Resurfacing: From apartment complexes and HOAs to hotels and community centers, we provide robust resurfacing solutions for high-traffic commercial pools throughout San Antonio. Our work ensures compliance with safety standards while creating an inviting, professional appearance for residents and guests.
  • Custom & Luxury Pool Resurfacing: For San Antonio homeowners desiring a truly unique aquatic experience, we offer custom resurfacing with premium finishes like Pebble Tec, quartz, or glass beads.
