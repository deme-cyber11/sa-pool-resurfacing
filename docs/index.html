Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about pool resurfacing in San Antonio

How much does pool resurfacing cost in San Antonio? +
Pool resurfacing typically costs $4,000-$15,000 depending on pool size and finish type. Basic plaster runs $4-6 per square foot, while premium pebble finishes cost $8-12 per square foot. We provide free estimates with exact pricing for your pool.
How long does pool resurfacing take? +
Most pool resurfacing projects take 5-7 days from start to finish. This includes draining, surface preparation, applying the new finish, filling, and initial chemical balancing.
When is the best time to resurface a pool in Texas? +
October through March is ideal. Cooler temperatures allow for better curing of the new surface, and you won't miss prime swimming season. However, we resurface pools year-round with proper precautions.
How long does pool plaster last in Texas? +
Standard pool plaster lasts 7-12 years in Texas's harsh climate. Pebble and quartz finishes last 15-20+ years due to their superior durability against sun exposure and hard water.
Pebble finish vs plaster - which is better? +
Pebble finishes cost more upfront ($8-12/sq ft vs $4-6/sq ft for plaster) but last twice as long and require less maintenance. For Texas pools exposed to intense sun and hard water, pebble often provides better long-term value.
