What Is Ernesto?

Ernesto, or professional pool resurfacing and replastering, is the comprehensive process of removing the aged, damaged finish from your swimming pool and applying a brand-new, durable surface. This involves stripping away old plaster, pebble, or tile, thoroughly preparing the substrate, and then meticulously applying a fresh layer. We utilize high-quality materials such as traditional white plaster, quartz aggregates for added durability and color, or exposed aggregate finishes like pebble tec, known for their extreme resilience and aesthetic appeal. The choice of material is tailored to your pool's needs and your aesthetic preferences, ensuring a smooth, watertight, and visually stunning finish that dramatically extends your pool's lifespan and enhances its overall appearance. It’s a specialized renovation that goes beyond simple repairs.
