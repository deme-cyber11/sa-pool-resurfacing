Frequently Asked Questions — Pool Resurfacing in Cibolo

How much does pool resurfacing cost in Cibolo, TX?

Pool resurfacing in Cibolo ranges from $3,500 for standard plaster to $12,000 for premium pebble finishes on large pools. The most popular option in Cibolo's newer neighborhoods is quartz aggregate at $5,500 to $8,500, which handles the area's water chemistry well and carries a longer warranty.

Do you serve the Copper Creek and Saddle Creek neighborhoods in Cibolo?

Yes. We serve all Cibolo neighborhoods including Copper Creek, Saddle Creek, Crossvine, Oak Meadows, and the newer subdivisions developing along FM-1103. Cibolo's rapid growth means many pools from the 2005 to 2015 building era are now due for their first resurfacing.

Is Cibolo water hard on pool finishes?

Cibolo draws from Guadalupe-Blanco River Authority sources with moderate to high mineral content. Combined with the intense summer heat, standard plaster typically shows wear within 8 to 12 years. We recommend quartz or pebble finishes for Cibolo homeowners looking for a longer-lasting solution.
