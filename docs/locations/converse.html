How much does pool resurfacing cost in Converse?

Pool resurfacing in Converse typically costs $3,500 to $12,000. White plaster is the most affordable option at $3,500 to $5,500, quartz aggregate runs $5,500 to $8,500, and pebble finishes like PebbleTec cost $7,000 to $12,000. The final price depends on your pool size, current surface condition, and chosen material. We offer free estimates.

My Converse pool is only 5 years old but looks rough. Why?

Many newer Converse pools were built during the recent housing boom and may have had basic plaster finishes installed to keep construction costs down. Combined with San Antonio area hard water and intense summer UV exposure, standard plaster can show etching, roughness, and discoloration in as little as 4 to 6 years. Upgrading to a quartz or pebble finish during resurfacing will give you a surface that lasts two to three times longer.
