Frequently Asked Questions — Pool Resurfacing in Kyle

How much does pool resurfacing cost in Kyle, TX?

Pool resurfacing in Kyle typically ranges from $3,500 to $11,500 depending on pool size and finish. Standard plaster starts around $3,500, quartz aggregate runs $5,500 to $8,500, and premium pebble finishes reach $11,500 for larger pools. We offer free on-site estimates with no obligation.

Do you serve the new subdivisions in Kyle?

Yes. We serve all of Kyle including Plum Creek, Waterleaf, Anthem, Crosswinds, and the newer developments along the SH-45 and SH-21 corridors. As one of the fastest-growing cities in the country, Kyle's housing boom means hundreds of new pools entering their first resurfacing cycle.

When should I resurface my pool in Kyle?

Most Kyle homeowners resurface in fall or early spring before peak swim season. With Hays County's long swim season running April through October, scheduling between November and February ensures you are swim-ready before the heat arrives.
