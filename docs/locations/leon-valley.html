Frequently Asked Questions — Pool Resurfacing in Leon Valley

What pool resurfacing options are available in Leon Valley?

Leon Valley homeowners can choose from standard white plaster ($3,500–$5,500), colored quartz aggregate ($5,500–$8,500), or premium pebble finishes ($7,000–$11,000). All options include a warranty and a free on-site estimate before any work begins.

How long does pool resurfacing take in Leon Valley?

Most Leon Valley pool resurfacing projects are completed in 5 to 8 days from drain to swim-ready. We schedule efficiently so your pool is back in service as quickly as possible.

Is Leon Valley water hard on pool surfaces?

Yes. Leon Valley draws from the San Antonio Water System, which sources from the Edwards Aquifer. The high calcium and magnesium content accelerates plaster deterioration. Quartz or pebble finishes are a smart investment here, lasting 15 to 25 years versus 8 to 12 for standard plaster.
