Our Process

How Pool Resurfacing Works in New Braunfels

1

Free Estimate

We visit your New Braunfels property, inspect the pool shell for cracks and delamination, and provide a detailed written quote ‚Äî no charge, no obligation.

2

Drain Pool

We safely drain your pool and haul away the water per Comal County regulations, protecting your yard and landscaping throughout the process.

3

Prep Surface

The old surface is chipped away, cracks are repaired, and the shell is sandblasted or acid-washed to create a clean, bonding-ready substrate.

4

Apply Finish

Our crew hand-applies your chosen plaster, pebble, or quartz finish using professional troweling techniques for even thickness and a flawless surface.

5

Refill & Balance

We fill the pool and carefully balance your water chemistry using startup protocols designed for the Edwards Aquifer's hard water, protecting your new finish from day one.
