How do I know if my Seguin pool needs resurfacing?

Common signs include rough or sandpaper-like texture on the pool walls, visible staining that chemical treatments cannot remove, small cracks or flaking plaster, and persistent algae growth in pitted areas. If your pool is more than 8 to 10 years old with a plaster finish, or you notice your feet getting scraped on the bottom, it is likely time for resurfacing.
