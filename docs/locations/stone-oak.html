Our Process

How Pool Resurfacing Works in Stone Oak

1

Free Estimate

We visit your Stone Oak property, conduct a thorough inspection of the pool, shell, coping, and deck, and deliver a detailed written estimate with finish samples to review at home.

2

Drain Pool

We drain your Stone Oak pool safely and in full compliance with San Antonio Water System regulations, protecting your landscaping and neighboring properties throughout.

3

Prep Surface

Old plaster is removed, structural cracks are repaired with flexible compounds, and the shell is prepared for the new surface coating using bonding agents designed for Texas conditions.

4

Apply Finish

Your chosen surface ‚Äî plaster, quartz aggregate, or pebble ‚Äî is expertly applied by our experienced crew with consistent thickness and a flawless finish throughout the pool shell.

5

Refill & Balance

We fill the pool and implement a full startup chemistry protocol, carefully monitoring calcium hardness, pH, and total alkalinity to protect your new surface from day one in Stone Oak's hard water environment.
