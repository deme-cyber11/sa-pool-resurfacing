If you've been fighting the same pool problems for two or three seasons — rough texture, staining that won't brush away, algae that keeps coming back two weeks after treatment — the issue is the surface, not the chemistry.

Universal City sits in the Tri-Cities area of northeast Bexar County, where alkaline Edwards Aquifer water and San Antonio's long, hot season work against pool surfaces year-round. Plaster surfaces in this area typically show serious degradation between the 10- and 15-year mark.
