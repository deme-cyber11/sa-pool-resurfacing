Section 6 — Service Agreement (Binding Terms)
1. Parties & Effective Date
Iron Tiger Digital and Keith Zars Pools
This Agreement is between Iron Tiger Digital (costa@irontigerleads.com · (225) 535-4918) and Keith Zars Pools, effective as of May 13, 2026. Iron Tiger Digital is the "Provider." Keith Zars Pools is the "Client."
2. Lead Definition
What constitutes a billable lead
A "Lead" is an inbound form or phone inquiry from Provider's lead generation site — a homeowner within the agreed service territory who is interested in new pool construction or major renovation. To be valid, a Lead must include a full name, a working phone number, and a service address within the territory.
3. Delivery Process
How leads are delivered
Each Lead is emailed to the address designated by Client at signing within 1 hour of receipt during business hours (Mon–Fri 8 AM–8 PM CT, Sat 10 AM–5 PM CT). The email includes name, phone, email, address, project description, and timestamp. Phone leads come through a dedicated call-tracking number — recordings available on request. Leads received after hours, on Sunday, or after 5 PM CT Saturday are delivered by 9 AM CT the next business morning.
4. Exclusivity
Each lead is delivered to Client only
Each Lead is delivered to Client only — Iron Tiger will not sell, share, or transfer it to any other party. Note: homeowners may contact other contractors on their own; Iron Tiger's exclusivity covers its own distribution only. All websites, domains, and rankings used to generate Leads remain Iron Tiger's property. This Agreement transfers no ownership in those assets, and termination gives Client no claim to them.
5. Service Territory
San Antonio metro, New Braunfels, Corpus Christi, and surrounding areas
Leads come from homeowners within 150 miles of San Antonio, Texas — including San Antonio metro, New Braunfels, Corpus Christi, Seguin, Boerne, Kerrville, and surrounding communities.
6. Fees & Payment
$500/completed job · Net-15 · Invoice on close report
Client pays $500 per completed job — defined as a signed contract from a Lead delivered under this Agreement. When a job closes, Client reports it to Iron Tiger. Iron Tiger invoices within 2 business days. Payment is due Net-15 via ACH, business check, QuickBooks/Bill.com, Stripe, or Zelle — whichever method is designated at signing. No setup fee, no retainer, no minimum volume. Invoices unpaid after 30 days may accrue 1.5% monthly interest. Provider may request a signed contract date or invoice number to verify job close upon dispute.
6A. Lead Disputes
3-business-day dispute window
To dispute a Lead, email costa@irontigerleads.com within 3 business days of delivery. Include the Lead's name, delivery date, and reason. Valid grounds: (a) phone number was non-functional at delivery, (b) address is outside the service territory, (c) clear duplicate of a prior Lead. Leads not disputed within this window are accepted as valid and billable upon job close.
7. No Conversion Guarantee
Provider delivers intent; Client closes the job
Iron Tiger delivers intent — it does not guarantee appointments, close rates, or signed contracts. Those outcomes are Client's responsibility.
8. Confidentiality
Lead data stays private
Both parties keep Lead data confidential — homeowner names, addresses, phone numbers, and project details. Client won't resell or distribute Lead data to third parties. Iron Tiger won't disclose Client's lead volume, close rates, or pricing to competitors. These obligations survive termination.
9. Term & Termination
Month-to-month · 30 days written notice
This Agreement is month-to-month, starting on the Effective Date. Either party can terminate with 30 days written notice by email. On termination, Iron Tiger stops delivering Leads. All invoices for Leads delivered before termination remain due. No early-termination penalties.
10. Limitation of Liability
Provider's maximum liability capped at invoice credits
Iron Tiger's maximum liability to Client won't exceed the total fees invoiced in the 3 months before the claim. Neither party is liable to the other for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages — including lost profits, lost revenue, or reputational harm — even if warned of the possibility.
11. Governing Law
State of Texas
This Agreement is governed by Texas law. Any disputes that can't be resolved informally will be handled in state or federal courts in Bexar County, Texas.
12. TCPA Compliance
Consent and telecommunications law
Iron Tiger generates Leads from opt-in forms with written consent disclosing that homeowners may be contacted by a licensed pool contractor — covering TCPA prior express written consent. Client is solely responsible for its own outreach practices and TCPA compliance, including FCC rules and the National Do Not Call Registry.
13. Entire Agreement
This document supersedes all prior discussions
This is the complete agreement between the parties on lead generation services. It replaces all prior discussions, emails, or verbal agreements. Changes must be in writing and signed by both parties.
14. Severability
Invalid provisions don't void the rest
If any part of this Agreement is found invalid or unenforceable, it will be modified just enough to make it valid — or removed if modification isn't possible. The rest of the Agreement stays in effect.
