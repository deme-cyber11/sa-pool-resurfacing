Section 3 — How It Works

  1. 1
    Homeowner submits via Iron Tiger lead gen site A homeowner searching for new pool construction or major renovation in the San Antonio / New Braunfels / Corpus Christi area finds alamopoolresurfacing.com, reads the content, and submits a contact form with their name, address, phone, and project description.
  2. 2
    Iron Tiger delivers a full lead packet within 1 hour Keith Zars Pools receives an email with the full lead record: name, address, phone, email (if provided), project description, and timestamp. No portal login — a shared read-only lead log and call recordings are available on request.
  3. 3
    Keith Zars follows up and closes the job The lead is exclusively yours from the moment it's delivered. Iron Tiger does not send the same lead to any other contractor. Your team calls, books the appointment, and closes the deal.
  4. 4
    Iron Tiger invoices $500 when the job closes When your team closes a job, report it to costa@irontigerleads.com. Iron Tiger sends an invoice within 2 business days. Keith Zars has 15 days to pay via ACH — no payment due on a lead unless the job closes.

Section 5 — Service Agreement (Binding Terms)
1. Parties & Effective Date
Iron Tiger Digital and Keith Zars Pools
This Lead Generation Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [Date] by and between Costa Demetral d/b/a Iron Tiger Digital, email: costa@irontigerleads.com, phone: (225) 535-4918 ("Provider") and Keith Zars Pools ("Client"). Provider and Client are each referred to individually as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties."
2. Lead Definition
What constitutes a billable lead
A "Lead" is defined as an inbound form submission or phone inquiry originated from Provider's lead generation website(s), from a homeowner located within the agreed service territory, expressing genuine interest in new pool construction or major pool renovation services. A valid Lead must include: (a) a full name, (b) a working contact phone number, and (c) a service address within the service territory.
3. Delivery Process
How leads are delivered
Provider will deliver each Lead to Client via email within one (1) hour of receipt during business hours (8 AM–8 PM CT Mon–Fri, 10 AM–5 PM CT Sat–Sun). Each delivery will include, to the extent available: full name, phone number, email address, service address, project description, and submission timestamp. Provider may, at its discretion, deliver leads received outside business hours on the following morning.
4. Exclusivity
Each lead is delivered to Client only
Once delivered, Provider will not sell, transfer, or share a Lead with any other party. Homeowners may contact other contractors independently — Provider's exclusivity covers only its own distribution. All websites, domains, and rankings used to generate Leads remain Provider's sole property — this Agreement transfers no ownership in Provider's digital assets, and termination grants Client no acquisition rights.
5. Service Territory
San Antonio metro, New Braunfels, Corpus Christi, and surrounding areas
Provider will deliver Leads originating from homeowners located within 100 miles of San Antonio, Texas, which includes but is not limited to: San Antonio metro, New Braunfels, Corpus Christi, Seguin, Boerne, Kerrville, and surrounding communities.
6. Fees & Payment
$500/completed job · Net-15 · Invoice on close report
Client agrees to pay Provider $500 (USD) per completed job. A 'completed job' means a signed contract with a Lead delivered under this Agreement. Client will report each closed job to Provider; Provider will issue an invoice within 2 business days of that report. Payment is due Net-15 from the invoice date via ACH bank transfer. No setup fee, no monthly retainer, no minimum volume commitment. Invoices unpaid after 30 days may accrue interest at 1.5% per month.
7. No Conversion Guarantee
Provider delivers intent; Client closes the job
Provider makes no representation regarding conversion rates. Appointments, signed contracts, and closed jobs are solely Client's responsibility.
8. Confidentiality
Lead data stays private
Each Party agrees to keep Lead data (including homeowner names, addresses, phone numbers, and project details) confidential and to use it solely for the purposes of this Agreement. Client will not resell, share, or distribute Lead data to any third party. Provider will not disclose Client's lead volume, close rates, or pricing to any competitor. This obligation survives termination of this Agreement.
9. Term & Termination
Month-to-month · 30 days written notice
This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues on a month-to-month basis. Either Party may terminate this Agreement by providing thirty (30) days written notice via email to the other Party's address on file. Upon termination: (a) Provider will cease delivering Leads, (b) all invoices for Leads delivered prior to termination remain due and payable per the payment terms, and (c) any pending disputes will be resolved under the normal process. There are no early-termination penalties.
