Section 4 — Lead Quality & Payment

A valid lead is defined as: an inbound form submission or phone call from a homeowner located within the service territory (San Antonio metro, New Braunfels, Corpus Christi, and surrounding areas within 100 miles), with a working phone number, expressing interest in a new pool build or major pool renovation.

Valid — you pay
Real name and working phone. Within territory. Inbound homeowner intent. Not a prior duplicate in this billing period.
Disputable — credit issued
Wrong/disconnected number. Address clearly outside 100-mile territory. Exact duplicate of a lead already delivered. Obvious spam or bot submission.
Not disputable
Lead didn't answer the phone. Lead chose a competitor. Lead changed their mind. Low budget. These are sales outcomes, not lead quality failures.
No conversion guarantee
Iron Tiger delivers qualified inbound intent. It does not guarantee that any lead will close — closing is Keith Zars' operation.
Dispute window: 14 calendar days from delivery. Flag via email to costa@irontigerleads.com with the lead name and reason. Credit is applied to the next weekly invoice — no cash refunds. Disputes not raised within 14 calendar days are considered accepted.

Iron Tiger Digital sends a weekly email invoice every Friday. Keith Zars pays via ACH bank transfer — same as paying any other vendor. Iron Tiger's routing and account number are included in the first invoice. No new accounts required, no credit card processing, no subscription fees on your end.
