Quartz Aggregate Finishes

Quartz pool finishes combine crushed quartz crystals with fortified polymer-modified cement for a surface that's smoother than pebble but more durable than plaster.

The quartz crystals create a beautiful shimmer in sunlight and come in a wide range of colors. They're exceptionally stain-resistant, which is key in San Antonio where mineral-rich well water is common.

  • Brilliant, sparkling appearance
  • Stain and chemical resistant
  • Smoother than pebble finishes
  • 10-year warranty

Tile & Coping

Waterline tile and coping are your pool's first line of defense against water damage. Cracked or missing tiles allow moisture to seep behind the finish, leading to delamination and costly repairs.

We install glass, ceramic, porcelain, and natural stone tile in patterns ranging from classic blue to custom mosaics. Our coping options include travertine, flagstone, brick, and poured concrete.

  • Glass, ceramic, and stone options
  • Custom mosaic designs available
  • Travertine and flagstone coping
  • Prevents water intrusion damage

Structural Repair
