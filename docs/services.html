Traditional Plaster & Quartz Finishes

For San Antonio, TX homeowners seeking a classic, smooth, and vibrant pool interior, our traditional plaster and quartz finishes remain incredibly popular choices. Plaster provides a timeless look, offering a clean, brilliant white or colored surface that beautifully reflects the Texas sky. Quartz finishes, on the other hand, incorporate durable quartz aggregates into the plaster, significantly enhancing longevity, stain resistance, and providing a subtle sparkle. Alamo Pool Resurfacing excels in applying these finishes with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring an even, enduring surface that stands up to constant use and the San Antonio climate. We prepare the existing surface thoroughly, apply the new material expertly, and oversee the curing process to deliver a stunning and long-lasting result that exceeds expectations.

Elegant Pebble & Exposed Aggregate Resurfacing

Elevate your pool's aesthetic and durability with our elegant pebble and exposed aggregate resurfacing options. These premium finishes are highly sought after by San Antonio, TX residents looking for a naturally textured, incredibly resilient, and visually captivating pool surface. Pebble finishes utilize small, smooth pebbles to create a rugged yet sophisticated look, offering superior resistance to chemical deterioration, staining, and etching compared to traditional plaster. Alamo Pool Resurfacing provides a wide array of pebble colors and sizes, allowing for complete customization to match your backyard's unique design theme. Our skilled technicians ensure a flawless application, creating a durable and slip-resistant surface that offers a luxurious feel underfoot and an extended lifespan for your beloved pool.

Comprehensive Crack Repair & Structural Integrity

Cracks and structural issues are not just cosmetic flaws; they can compromise the integrity and safety of your swimming pool. For San Antonio, TX pools, temperature fluctuations and ground movement can often lead to such problems. Alamo Pool Resurfacing offers comprehensive crack repair services, addressing everything from hairline fissures to more significant structural damage that could lead to leaks and further deterioration. Our process involves expert assessment to identify the root cause of the cracks, followed by specialized repair techniques such as staples, epoxy injections, or fiberglass overlays. By meticulously restoring your pool's structural integrity before any resurfacing, we ensure that the new finish adheres properly and lasts for many years, protecting your investment and preventing future, costly repairs.

Coping & Tile Replacement Services

Beyond the interior surface, the coping and tiles play a crucial role in both the aesthetics and functionality of your pool. Deteriorated coping or cracked tiles can detract from your pool's beauty and pose safety hazards. Alamo Pool Resurfacing provides expert coping and tile replacement services for San Antonio, TX homeowners. Whether you desire a fresh, modern look with new porcelain or mosaic tiles, or need to replace damaged coping with durable flagstone or concrete pavers, our team delivers precision craftsmanship. We carefully remove old materials, prepare the surfaces, and install new coping and tiles with expert grouting and sealing. This not only enhances the visual appeal of your pool but also protects its edges and structure, providing a complete and revitalized finish.
