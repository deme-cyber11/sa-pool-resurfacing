Pool Resurfacing Services

Premium pool finishes built to withstand San Antonio's intense Texas heat

Pool Replastering

Pool replastering is the most popular and affordable way to restore your pool's interior surface. Our classic white plaster provides a smooth, clean finish that looks beautiful in any backyard. In San Antonio's climate, where the intense Texas sun and hard Hill Country water take their toll, quality plaster application is essential for longevity.

We use only premium plaster materials mixed to exact specifications, ensuring a durable finish that resists staining and etching. Our experienced crews hand-apply every surface for a smooth, consistent result.

  • Classic smooth white finish
  • Most affordable resurfacing option ($4-6 per sq ft)
  • Available in white, gray, and blue tints
  • 7-12 year lifespan with proper maintenance
  • Ideal for budget-conscious homeowners

Pebble Finishes

Pebble finishes are the premium choice for San Antonio pool owners who want maximum durability and stunning aesthetics. Using natural pebble aggregates, these finishes create a beautiful textured surface that stands up to the harshest Texas conditions — blazing sun, hard water, and extreme temperature swings.

Popular with homeowners in Alamo Heights, Stone Oak, and Boerne, pebble finishes offer a natural look that complements Hill Country landscapes beautifully.

  • 15-20+ year lifespan — the longest-lasting option
  • Natural stone texture with dozens of color blends
  • Superior resistance to chemicals, staining, and etching
  • Ideal for San Antonio's hard water conditions
  • Investment pricing: $8-12 per square foot

Quartz Finishes

Quartz finishes combine the beauty of sparkling aggregate with excellent durability. The crushed quartz crystals create a smooth yet slip-resistant surface that shimmers in the San Antonio sunshine. Available in a wide range of colors, quartz finishes let you customize your pool's appearance while maintaining long-term performance.

  • Sparkling, reflective surface
  • Smoother than pebble, more durable than plaster
  • Excellent stain resistance
  • 12-15+ year lifespan
  • Mid-range pricing: $6-10 per square foot

Pool Tile & Coping Repair

Cracked or missing waterline tiles and damaged coping stones are more than cosmetic issues — they can lead to structural problems. Our tile and coping repair services restore your pool's finished edge while preventing water intrusion. We match existing tile patterns or help you choose a fresh new look.

  • Waterline tile replacement and repair
  • Coping stone restoration and replacement
  • Color matching to existing materials
  • Often combined with full resurfacing for a complete refresh

Pool Deck Resurfacing

San Antonio pool decks take a beating from the Texas sun, reaching temperatures that can burn bare feet. Our cool deck coatings and textured finishes not only look great but stay comfortable even in 100°F heat. We offer a variety of colors and textures to complement your pool and home.

  • Cool deck coatings that reduce surface temperature
  • Textured, slip-resistant finishes
  • Stamped and decorative concrete overlays
  • Crack repair and leveling
  • Multiple color and pattern options

Color Matching

Need to match your existing pool finish or complement your backyard design? Our color matching service ensures a seamless blend. We carry an extensive range of plaster tints, pebble blends, and quartz colors to achieve exactly the look you want.

Commercial Pool Resurfacing

We serve HOAs, hotels, apartment complexes, and municipal pools across the San Antonio metro area. Our commercial resurfacing services are designed for minimal downtime and maximum durability. We understand the unique requirements of commercial pool operations and work within your schedule.

  • HOA and community pool specialists
  • Hotel and resort pool resurfacing
  • Municipal and public pool services
  • Flexible scheduling to minimize disruption
  • Commercial-grade materials for high-traffic environments
