Our Services

Pool resurfacing, replastering, and renovation for San Antonio homeowners.

Complete Pool Resurfacing & Replastering Solutions

We handle everything — from essential replastering to full renovations with premium finishes. Whether your pool needs a minor refresh or a complete overhaul, you get one experienced team managing the entire project from drain to swim-ready.

Plaster & Quartz Finishes

Our traditional plaster and quartz finishes are the most popular choices for San Antonio homeowners. Plaster delivers a clean, timeless white surface at an accessible price point. Quartz aggregate finishes add durability and stain resistance — particularly valuable in San Antonio's hard Edwards Aquifer water — with a subtle sparkle and a 12–15 year lifespan. We apply both with meticulous prep work and oversee the full curing process.

  • White plaster: $4–6 per sq ft. Classic look, 8–12 year lifespan.
  • Quartz aggregate: $6–9 per sq ft. Superior scale resistance for SA's hard water, 12–15 year lifespan.

Pebble & Exposed Aggregate Finishes

Pebble finishes (PebbleTec, PebbleSheen) are the premium tier — naturally textured, visually striking, and built to outlast plaster in San Antonio's mineral-rich water. They resist chemical deterioration, staining, and etching at a level plaster simply can't match. We offer a wide range of pebble colors and sizes for full customization.

  • Pebble Tec / PebbleSheen: $8–12 per sq ft. 15–20 year lifespan, best choice for SA's hard water.
  • Glass bead finishes: Available for custom luxury projects. Brilliant reflective finish.

Tile & Coping Replacement

Waterline tile and coping take the most visible abuse — calcium scaling, UV fading, and physical wear. We replace deteriorated coping and tiles with durable materials that complement your new surface finish. Options include porcelain, ceramic, mosaic, flagstone, and concrete pavers. All grouting and sealing is included.

Structural Crack Repair

San Antonio's limestone and caliche soil shifts with moisture changes, causing cracks in pool shells more often than most Texas markets. We assess every crack — distinguishing surface-only from structural — before any resurfacing begins. Structural repairs use staples, epoxy injections, or fiberglass overlays as appropriate. The assessment is included at no extra charge with every job.

Resurfacing over an unrepaired structural crack is a temporary fix that fails within 1–2 years. We won't do it.

Commercial Pool Resurfacing

We work with hotels, HOAs, apartment complexes, community centers, and schools throughout the San Antonio area. Commercial projects require coordination to minimize downtime and meet health code requirements. We use industrial-grade materials and schedule efficiently to get high-traffic pools back online as fast as possible.
